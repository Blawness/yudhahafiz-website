# Projects by Yudha Hafiz: Real Problems, Real Solutions

Every project Yudha Hafiz builds starts with one question: *"What problem are we actually solving?"*

Here's a look at some of the most impactful work.

## Document Management System (DMS)

**The Problem:** A mid-sized company had files scattered across email, USB drives, and shared folders. Finding a document took an average of 15 minutes. Compliance audits were a nightmare.

**The Solution:** A full-featured DMS built with Next.js, TypeScript, and PostgreSQL. Features included:
- Advanced search with filters
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Activity audit logs
- Bulk file operations
- Version history

**The Result:** 
- 80% reduction in document retrieval time
- Full audit compliance achieved
- Onboarded 50+ users in week one

## AI-Powered Customer Support Bot

**The Problem:** A growing e-commerce client was receiving 300+ support tickets per week. 70% were repetitive questions about orders, shipping, and returns.

**The Solution:** Custom AI chatbot powered by GPT-4, trained on the client's product catalog and FAQ database. Deployed on WhatsApp and web chat with:
- Smart escalation to human agents
- CRM integration for order lookups
- Multi-language support (Indonesian + English)

**The Result:**
- 70% ticket deflection rate
- Average response time: 30 seconds (down from 4 hours)
- Support team refocused on complex, high-value cases

## Business Automation Dashboard

**The Problem:** A business owner was manually moving data between 5 different tools — ERP, CRM, accounting software, marketplace, and analytics. This took 20+ hours per week.

**The Solution:** A central automation hub that:
- Connected all tools via their APIs
- Automated data sync on a schedule
- Provided real-time monitoring and alerts
- Offered one-click manual triggers

**The Result:**
- 20 hours saved per week
- Zero data entry errors
- Owner had full visibility across all business operations
